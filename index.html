Peleton rides in Stockholm

Explore Stockholm's urban expanse on our group rides that combine city cycling with skill enhancement. These rides are designed to help you master different techniques, push your speed limits, and refine your cycling proficiency.

We're committed to upholding cyclist etiquette and safety during our rides, fostering an environment of respect and adherence to common biking rules. Let's unite our strengths and grow collectively through these shared experiences.

Join us as we pedal towards becoming more adept cyclists, while savoring the urban beauty that surrounds us.
