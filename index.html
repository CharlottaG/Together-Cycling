Peleton rides in Stockholm

Explore Stockholm's Urban Beauty with Our Peleton Rides! Discover the heart of Stockholm through our group rides, seamlessly blending city cycling with skill improvement. Elevate your technique, embrace safety and cyclist etiquette, and join us in becoming better cyclists together.

We started our journey in 2005 with little knowledge but an eager to master bike riding, now we craft rides and build our own bikes. Join our adventure!
